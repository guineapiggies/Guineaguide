Welcome to GuineaGuide! this is a basic guide for anyone who is new to being a "Guinea Pig Parent", this guide has some of the best information witten by an actual guinea pig owner! This guide website goes over lots of the basic guinea pig information, and clears up lots of common misconceptions that may be had when owning a Guinea Pig.

My website is intended for Guinea Pigs ONLY, and if you have any questions about any other types of rodents. this is not the place to be! This is specifically a guinea pig website!
